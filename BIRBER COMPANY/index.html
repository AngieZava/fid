About us:

We are a nonprofit organization encouraging the public's education on parrots and small birds kept as pets.

Our Goal:

A lot of the time, people may buy birds as ornaments, not doing the proper research on how to take care of these delicate animals, so we've put together a basic fact hub, with the goal to teach everyone how special and important these animals are.
